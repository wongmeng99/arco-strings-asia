Suzuki Violin Lessons

Group lessons are a unique and fun aspect of the Suzuki Violin Method, and they are a wonderful opportunity to motivate students.

In group lessons, students will review music vocabulary, refine techniques, work and play together as an ensemble, develop leadership skills, and have a good time!
